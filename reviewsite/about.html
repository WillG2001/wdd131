About my rating scale

The grading system, at least here in the US, doesn’t make a whole lot of sense to me. You’re telling me that something 75% correct constitutes an “average” grade? Maybe it works for school assignments, but for media, I use a different scale: a simple, 10 point system where each point value summarizes how I feel about a piece of media. In my system, a 5 is an average score, right in the middle of the scale. Here’s a breakdown of what each point value represents.

10 - Just about perfect in every way. Pieces of media that score a ten are few and far between, and have little to no flaws whatsoever. Tens are not only excellent, but special. These pieces of media make me feel something beyond just “this is pretty cool”. They make me think, they make me feel a range of emotions, and they make me want to share them with the rest of the world.

9 - Outstanding. An excellent piece of media with a few flaws.

8 - Great! More flawed than a nine, but still a very enjoyable experience.

7 - Solid. Something that’s good in most aspects, with a few issues here and there.

6 - OK. Not amazing, but not bad either. A somewhat flawed piece of media.

5 - Average. A piece of media that isn’t bad, per se, but also doesn’t have much going for it.

4 - Meh… slightly below average. A lot of flaws and nothing special.

3 - Subpar. A very flawed piece of media that isn’t very enjoyable.

2 - Bad. Way too many mistakes were made when making this piece of media, but at least they tried. Just not something really worth spending time with.

1 - Atrocious. Not only is it bad, but it’s offensively bad. It’s like they didn’t even try, or were intentionally trying to create a monster. The only thing separating this from a zero is that it can indeed be considered art, even if it absolutely stinks.

0 - Worthless. Never, EVER, under ANY circumstances, consume this piece of media. This score is reserved only for media that is intentionally offensive, extremely inappropriate, or was made unethically. Zeroes shouldn’t even be considered art in my opinion. Nothing I’ll ever bother to write a review for will score a zero.
